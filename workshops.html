CULTURAL WORKSHOPS

A crucial pillar of our Institute. Move beyond grammar to immerse yourself in the arts, gastronomy, and society through interactive cultural workshop timelines.

Workshop Timelines

Interactive cultural workshop timelines are mapped directly to your CEFR proficiency level.

Weeks 1-4 (A1 Level)

Daily Customs & Etiquette

Understand the nuances of greetings, body language, and dining etiquette. Essential sociological context for beginners.

Weeks 5-8 (B1 Level)

Modern Pop Culture

An interactive timeline analyzing contemporary music, cinema, and slang. We break down media clips with native speaker instructors.

Weeks 9-12 (C1 Level)

Historical Debates

Advanced workshops focusing on historical turning points. Students participate in live debates using advanced idiomatic expressions.

  • Deep dive into cultural idioms
