CURRICULUM PATHWAYS

A clean, multilingual global site layout mapping out curriculum pathways (A1 through C2 levels) to guide your journey from complete beginner to native-level fluency.

Mastery Levels

Our courses align with the internationally recognized CEFR framework, mapping out curriculum pathways clearly.

A

Basic User

A1 - A2

  • Introduce yourself and use basic greetings.
  • Understand sentences and frequently used expressions.
  • Communicate in simple, routine tasks.
B

Independent

B1 - B2

  • Handle most situations while traveling.
  • Produce simple, connected text on familiar topics.
  • Understand the main ideas of complex text.
C

Proficient

C1 - C2

  • Express ideas fluently and spontaneously.
  • Use language flexibly for social and professional purposes.
  • Summarize information from different spoken sources.

The Progression Map

1

Diagnostic Assessment

Before starting, students complete level placement exams via their dashboard to ensure they are mapped to the correct starting tier.

2

Core Language Acquisition

Weekly immersion sessions with native speakers. Students download homework/audio practice files to reinforce daily learning.

3

Cultural Integration

Interactive cultural workshop timelines are introduced at the B1 level, bridging the gap between vocabulary and societal context.

4

Evaluation & Advancement

View digital course grade books and track class attendance records. Final exams qualify students to advance to the next CEFR level.
