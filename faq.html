Have questions about our curriculum pathways, cultural workshops, or dashboard features? Find all your answers right here.

Curriculum

Everything you need to know about our mapped progression and proficiency levels.

How do I know my starting proficiency level?
We require all new students to complete level placement exams. These diagnostics ensure you are correctly mapped to our curriculum pathways (A1 through C2 levels) to start your language journey appropriately.
What does mapping out curriculum pathways mean?
Our institute provides a clean, multilingual global site layout mapping out curriculum pathways (A1 through C2 levels). This means your entire progression is structured logically from beginner to advanced without any guesswork.
Can I switch my language track later?
Yes, you can easily change tracks. Simply access your dashboard to adjust your settings, and you will be prompted to take a new placement exam for the new language.

Portal Features

Learn how to navigate your dashboard and access digital resources.

How do I check my grades and progress?
Inside your student account, you have access to a dedicated dashboard. Here, you can view digital course grade books and track class attendance records instantly.
Where can I find materials to practice outside of class?
The portal is designed for language students to actively engage with materials. You can easily download homework/audio practice files directly from your dashboard's resource center.

Faculty & Culture

Answers regarding our teaching staff and cultural immersion sessions.

Who will be teaching my classes?
You will be taught exclusively by professionals. You can browse our directory to view native speaker instructor profile blocks, which highlight their teaching methodologies and cultural backgrounds.
What are cultural workshops?
We believe language requires context. Our curriculum features interactive cultural workshop timelines that dive into the history, gastronomy, and art of the language you are studying.

Plans & Formatting

Guidance on selecting the right course package and format.

How do I choose between standard classes and bootcamps?
We have made this simple. You can view our dedicated course comparison cards to clearly see the differences between our standard weekly pacing and our intensive bootcamp formatting.
Do I have to pay extra for the dashboard features?
No, full dashboard access is included in all plans. You will always be able to complete level placement exams, view digital course grade books, and download audio practice files at no extra cost.

Still Have Questions?

If you couldn't find the answer you were looking for regarding our curriculum, dashboard, or pricing, our support team is ready to assist you.
