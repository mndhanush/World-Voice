Curriculum Pathways

Structured progression mapping out curriculum pathways (A1 through C2 levels) to guide your language mastery journey.

A1 - A2 (Beginner)

Build foundational vocabulary, essential grammar, and basic conversational skills necessary for daily cultural interactions.

Popular

B1 - B2 (Intermediate)

Achieve conversational fluency, understand complex texts, and engage actively in our interactive cultural workshops.

C1 - C2 (Advanced)

Master subtleties, idiomatic expressions, and professional-level proficiency equivalent to a native speaker.
